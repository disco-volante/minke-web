Welcome to Minke.

Minke, a simple, yet powerful shopping assistant. It has a web interface maintained here and an Android app which can be found here here. Minke focuses on bringing you accurate and relevant information about products and stores through the use of the ZXing Barcode Scanner. Furthermore it provides additional shopping information such as which is the best store to do shopping for your shopping list and allows you to compare products across stores.
